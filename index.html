Moana is about a little girl who has always been fond of the sea but her dad opposes of the idea of the ocean. This adventurous girl was sent out far at sea for a mission to save the world with a demigod named Maui


Important Characters

Moana, main character who set sail together with Maui.


Maui, demigod of the wind and sea, hero of men.





Te Fiti, she is basically mother nature.


Te Ka, the main antagonist, a lava monster. Te Fiti turned into Te Ka after getting her heart stolen.


Moana's pet, Heihei, clueless but useful chicken.
